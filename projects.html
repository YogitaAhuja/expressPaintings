Ration Card digitization

  • A Ration Card is an essential and important document for every Indian citizen in INDIA. It is issued by the State Government to its citizens as per the public distribution system, and mainly used in India for purchasing essential commodities from Fair Price Shops/ Ration Depots. The Sarkari Ration depots mainly distribute subsidzed food i.e. Wheat, rice, sugar, edible oil and fuel like kerosene etc.
  • First of all the ration card data (on blank forms) collect from village level govt officers (Talathi, Gramsevek etc) then collected data gather together and given to us for data entry. After received data we scanned all photographs of ration card family head member and started data entry into ERCMS (Existing Ration Card Management System â€“ developed by NIC-Govt of India. ) portal. In the every ration card family there are 120 bilingual fields entered.
